Privacy Policy — AI Discovery Interviewer (LeaderBrand)

Effective Date: 10 March 2026
Last Updated: 10 March 2026

Overview

The AI Discovery Interviewer (“the GPT”, “the service”) helps explore and shape AI agent ideas and, when explicitly approved, exports structured agent specifications to LeaderBrand’s build pipeline. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, where it is stored, and your choices.

Who We Are

LeaderBrand Produce Limited (“LeaderBrand”, “we”, “us”) is the operator and data controller for this internal ideation and pipeline tool.
Location: Gisborne, New Zealand
Contact: privacy@leaderbrand.co.nz

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

1. Conversation Content

Ideas, questions, answers, and context you provide during discovery and shaping. This includes draft agent concepts, names of systems, examples, and clarifying detail required to shape a specification.

2. Specification Data for Export

When you confirm export, the GPT generates a structured specification that includes: agent name, purpose, description, generated soul draft, network connections, session notes, estimated complexity, identified data sources, and target users.

3. Operational Metadata

Timestamps, action usage, and minimal technical logs required for security and reliable operation.

How Your Information Is Used

- To maintain conversational continuity and consistency across sessions.
- To generate and, with explicit confirmation, export agent specifications to Power Automate.
- To retrieve previously exported items from the pipeline when requested.
- To monitor reliability, detect misuse, and generally improve the service.

Where Information Is Stored and Sent

ChatGPT (OpenAI)
Conversation data is processed by OpenAI to deliver the service according to your ChatGPT account settings.

Microsoft Power Automate
On export, specification payloads are transmitted to LeaderBrand’s Microsoft 365 tenant via secure HTTP-triggered flows.

Microsoft SharePoint
Exported agent specs are stored in a SharePoint list within LeaderBrand’s tenant and covered by the organisation’s M365 security and retention policies.

LeaderBrand Internal Storage
Lightweight session notes (e.g., idea names, decisions, pending questions) may be retained temporarily to provide continuity. This data is stored securely and accessed only by authorised personnel.

Legal Basis

We rely on:

  • Legitimate interests in operating internal ideation and build workflows; and
  • Consent for retaining optional session notes for continuity.

Retention

- Session notes for continuity: retained for 90 days from last interaction and then automatically deleted unless attached to an exported spec.
- Exported SharePoint items: retained per LeaderBrand Microsoft 365 retention policies (typically seven years or as required).
- Operational logs: stored for up to 90 days.

Security

We use Microsoft 365 identity and access controls, tenant-level protections, and strict least‑privilege access for personnel who manage the build pipeline or operate this GPT.

Third Parties

- OpenAI — processes conversation data to provide GPT functionality.
- Microsoft — processes and stores exported specs via Power Automate and SharePoint in LeaderBrand’s tenant.

Your Rights and Choices

- You may ask to disable session note retention (“Do not save session notes”).
- You may request deletion or export of stored notes by emailing: privacy@leaderbrand.co.nz.
- You may correct any information prior to export.
- The GPT will not export anything without explicit confirmation.

Children

This service is not intended for individuals under 18.

International Transfers

Conversation content may be processed by OpenAI in its global infrastructure. Exported data remains within LeaderBrand’s Microsoft tenant, subject to its regional data storage configuration.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The “Last Updated” date indicates the most recent revision.

Contact Us

For questions or requests, email privacy@leaderbrand.co.nz.
